# 08. Phase 6: Agency Scale And Advanced Product

## Phase Goal

Make the product powerful enough for an agency managing many clients.

The tool should eventually replace or reduce reliance on:

- manual crawl spreadsheets.
- manual GSC exports.
- manual GA4 exports.
- manual issue sheets.
- manual monthly reports.
- generic AI prompts.
- scattered notes.
- separate rank tracking where possible.

## Agency Workspace

Workspace should show:

- active projects.
- projects needing crawl.
- projects needing audit.
- projects needing profile approval.
- projects with critical issues.
- projects with ranking drops.
- projects with traffic drops.
- reports due.
- failed integrations.
- failed scheduled jobs.
- fixes needing verification.
- owner workload.
- top opportunities.

## Client Health Score

Create a health score based on:

- technical health.
- crawl reliability.
- critical/high issues.
- traffic trend.
- keyword trend.
- conversion trend.
- report readiness.
- integration freshness.
- unresolved issue age.

The score must be explainable.

Do not show a mysterious score without breakdown.

## Work Queues

Create queues:

- My issues.
- Critical fixes.
- Content tasks.
- Developer tasks.
- Reports due.
- Verification needed.
- Integration failures.
- Opportunity backlog.

Each queue should support:

- filters.
- sorting.
- bulk assign.
- bulk status update.
- due dates.
- notes.

## Monitoring Alerts

Alerts:

- crawl failed.
- audit failed.
- GSC sync failed.
- GA4 sync failed.
- important page noindex.
- important page 404/500.
- sitemap missing.
- traffic dropped.
- clicks dropped.
- ranking dropped.
- conversions dropped.
- title/meta drifted.
- critical issue opened.
- report overdue.

Alerts should include:

- severity.
- owner.
- affected project.
- affected page/keyword.
- evidence.
- action.

## Team And Permissions

Auth can be added later, but product thinking should allow:

- agency owner.
- SEO lead.
- SEO analyst.
- content strategist.
- developer.
- client viewer.

## Client Sharing

Later, build:

- shareable report webpage.
- token/password access.
- client-friendly view.
- internal-only sections.
- comments.
- approvals.
- report archive.

This can be better than PDF.

## Big Product Rule

Every advanced feature must connect to:

- evidence.
- issue.
- opportunity.
- report.
- monitoring.
- client goal.

If it does not connect to one of these, it is likely a distraction.

## Completion Criteria

This phase is complete only when:

- SEO lead can manage many clients from workspace.
- analysts can see assigned work.
- recurring monitoring creates actionable alerts.
- reports are generated from real data.
- opportunities turn into tasks.
- clients can understand progress.

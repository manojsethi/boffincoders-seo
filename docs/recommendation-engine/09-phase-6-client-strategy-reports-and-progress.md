# 09. Phase 6: Client Strategy Reports And Progress

## Phase Goal

Make recommendations and fix plans visible in client-ready reports.

Reports should communicate strategy, progress, and next actions.

## Client Report Must Answer

- What did we find?
- What did we fix?
- What improved?
- What still needs work?
- What are we doing next?
- What does the client need to approve or provide?

## Report Sections

Add recommendation-driven sections:

- Top recommended actions.
- Fixes completed.
- Fixes verified.
- Open critical/high recommendations.
- Keyword/page opportunities.
- Content improvements planned.
- Technical fixes planned.
- Client approvals needed.
- Next 30-day plan.

## Client Language

Use plain language.

Avoid:

- raw rule ids.
- overwhelming technical tables.
- unexplained metrics.
- uncertain AI claims.

Use:

- evidence.
- practical impact.
- clear next step.
- owner.
- expected outcome.

## Internal Report View

Internal reports can include:

- full evidence.
- rule ids.
- AI confidence.
- data gaps.
- internal notes.
- rejected recommendations.
- work queue details.

## Progress Tracking

Track:

- recommendations created.
- recommendations approved.
- recommendations implemented.
- recommendations verified.
- keyword movement.
- CTR movement.
- traffic/conversion movement.
- issue count trend.

## Validation Checklist

Do not mark complete until:

- client report includes approved recommendations.
- internal-only recommendations are hidden from client view.
- verified fixes appear in progress section.
- next actions are clear.
- client can understand what is happening without SEO expertise.
- self-audit is written.

# 06. Phase 5: Content Briefs And External AI Writing

## Purpose

Create evidence-backed content briefs from approved recommendations.

External AI may help write, but only after the SEO logic and evidence are clear.

## Must Read

- `docs/recommendation-engine/07-phase-4-content-briefs-and-external-ai-writing.md`
- `docs/ai-implementation/08-phase-5-report-assist-and-client-language.md`
- `docs/recommendation-engine/05-phase-2-keyword-page-fit-analysis.md`
- `docs/recommendation-engine/06-phase-3-page-content-analysis.md`

## Build Scope

Create content briefs from:

- approved content recommendations.
- keyword-page fit analysis.
- content analysis.
- GSC/GA4 evidence.
- project goals.
- page role.

## Brief Must Include

- target page or new page.
- target keyword(s).
- search intent.
- funnel stage.
- page objective.
- title suggestions.
- H1 suggestion.
- required sections.
- FAQ ideas.
- internal links to add.
- internal links from other pages.
- schema recommendation.
- CTA recommendation.
- trust/proof requirements.
- evidence references.

## External AI Use

External AI can generate:

- outline expansion.
- draft copy.
- title/meta alternatives.
- FAQ drafts.
- client-facing explanation.

Do not use local tiny models for final content writing.

Do not publish AI output automatically.

Analyst review is mandatory.

## Validation

Validate:

- generated brief references real evidence.
- no invented facts.
- brief links back to recommendation.
- draft status and approval status are clear.
- external AI usage and cost are logged.
